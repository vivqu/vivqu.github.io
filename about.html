About

I am a software engineer and writer. I am currently at Meta where I build iOS infrastructure for mobile apps. Previously, I was Head of Engineering at Echo AI and scaled the mobile apps to international markets at Pinterest. I got my B.S.E. in computer science at Princeton University.

I currently live in London with my husband and our border collie, Knuckles. Before that, we lived in Oakland and San Francisco for a decade. We also spent a year abroad in Tokyo. I was born and raised in the magical swamplands of Orlando, Florida.
