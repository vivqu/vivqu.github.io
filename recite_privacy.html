Privacy Policy

This page was last updated on Feb 12, 2023.

This policy describes the information that I, Vivian Qu, may receive from Recite chrome extension users and how that information is handled.

What I want, what’s most important to me, is:

  • To enable usage of Google spreadsheets in order to display quotes.
  • To be able to identify and fix crashes & bugs in the extension.
  • To log traffic to this website and view in aggregate.

In other words, I don’t want any private information — except for what is necessary information to be able to identify and fix crashes & bugs, and things like how many times the extension has been downloaded.

Much of this policy was proudly copied directly from the Information Superhighway policy. I appreciated the conciseness and readability of IS's policy and thought it worthy of stealing.

Google OAuth2 Access

Authenticating with your Google account is only required for the purposes of determining permissions and access to a Google spreadsheet. No data is collected on your email or spreadsheet information. Google does collect anonymized information on traffic, errors, and media latency for APIs.

Crash Logs

Currently, there is no specific libraries or APIs that are being used to log crashes. This is something that I may add in the future and the data will only be collected to faciliate fixing bugs and issues.

Website Logging and Trackers

Visits to the website are logged via anonymized Google Analytics. I am only interested in basic information like number of visitors to a particular blog post. You can read more about the website's general privacy policy here.
